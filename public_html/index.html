Endowed by Nature

BitAmulets are not just icon. Each one is exclusively generated for each user based on:

  • the name
  • the year of birth
  • the date and time of the request
  • the position of all stars in our solar system and their distance from earth in relation to the exact position of the user on earth.

Power of our solar system

We have used the power of our solar system to work for the interest of the users. Apparently, there is no direct, significantly verified relation between the position of the planets and our luck or destiny. BitAmulets do not, under no circumstances, support such a thesis. We justify the chances that our life is affected by something - if anything but pure luck - and we try to combat this unpredictability with the most powerful constant.

Solar System Positioning

Accepting that the solar system could have any affect to our life, we leverage this chance with the most appropriate and scientific manner.
